About Books For Girls

This site is a personal project to provide resources on children's literature for and about young girls. As the parent of a young daughter, I have observed that girls are often relegated to the background (or not present at all) in so many children's books (especially the classics). Think Frog and Toad or Max in Where the Wild Things Are.

I grew tired of reading my daughter books about firefighters just geared towards boys and decided to read Molly, by Golly!: The Legend of Molly Williams, America's First Female Firefighter instead!
