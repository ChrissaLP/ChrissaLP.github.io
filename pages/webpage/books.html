Review: Corduroy Takes a Bow

A New Look at a Classic

November 20, 2018

The release of Corduroy Takes A Bow marks the 50th anniversary of the publication of the classic book Corduroy by Don Freeman. What was unusual about the original book was not its eponymous main character - a teddy bear named Corduroy - but its heroine, Lisa, an African-American child who rescues Corduroy from a department store.
